## Why Fort Pierce Keeps Notaries Busy

- **A historic, established community.** Longtime residents often need estate planning documents — powers of attorney, trusts, healthcare directives.
- **Active waterfront and residential real estate.** Fort Pierce's property market keeps closing-related notarizations steady.
- **Lawnwood Regional Medical Center.** Hospital bedside visits for medical and financial powers of attorney are a regular request.
- **A growing small business community**, particularly around the revitalized downtown, generating LLC and contract notarizations.

## Documents Commonly Notarized in Fort Pierce

- Real estate deeds and closing documents
- Powers of attorney and healthcare surrogate designations
- Hospital and bedside notarizations
- Affidavits and sworn statements
- Business formation documents

For more on which documents typically require a notary, see our [full document guide](/blog/documents-that-require-a-notary).
