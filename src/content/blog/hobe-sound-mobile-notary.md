## Why Hobe Sound Residents Need a Notary

- **A significant retiree community.** Powers of attorney, healthcare directives, and trust documents are a steady need.
- **A quieter, close-knit residential character**, where home visits are often preferred over travel to a bank or office.
- **Seasonal residents**, needing documents notarized before or after time spent elsewhere during the year.
- **Nearby natural areas and a slower pace of life** that make mobile, in-home appointments a natural fit for the community.

## Documents Commonly Notarized in Hobe Sound

- Powers of attorney and healthcare surrogate designations
- Trust and estate planning documents
- Real estate deeds and closing documents
- Affidavits and sworn statements
- Travel consent forms, for seasonal travel
