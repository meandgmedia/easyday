Indian River County is home base — which means whether you're in downtown Vero Beach, out toward Fellsmere's agricultural communities, or up in Sebastian near the county line, you're covered by the fastest response times in the entire service area.

## The Full County, Covered

Mobile notary service reaches every part of Indian River County:

- **Vero Beach**, including the barrier island and mainland neighborhoods
- **Sebastian**, toward the county's northern edge
- **Fellsmere**, further inland
- **Unincorporated areas** throughout the county

Given the county's relatively compact size, most locations fall within the closest, lowest-cost travel tier.

## What It Costs

Most of Indian River County falls within the 0–15 mile tier centered on Vero Beach:

| Area | Travel Fee | Per-Stamp Fee |

| Sebastian | $55 | $10 |

## Why Indian River County Keeps Notaries Busy

- **A large retiree and seasonal resident population.** Estate planning documents — powers of attorney, trusts, healthcare directives — are a consistent need countywide.
- **An active real estate market**, particularly along the barrier island and in Vero Beach's established neighborhoods.
- **Cleveland Clinic Indian River and other medical facilities**, generating hospital bedside notarization requests.
- **A growing agricultural and small business sector** around Fellsmere and the county's western communities.

## Documents Commonly Notarized Countywide

- Powers of attorney and healthcare surrogate designations
- Real estate deeds and closing documents
- Hospital and bedside notarizations
- Trust and estate planning documents
- Business formation documents and affidavits

Our [full document guide](/blog/documents-that-require-a-notary) covers the broader range of what typically needs a notary's seal.

## Being Based Here Actually Matters

Operating from within Indian River County itself — rather than driving in from another county — means genuinely fast turnaround for local residents. Same-day and even same-hour appointments are realistic here in a way that's harder to promise for areas further from home base.
