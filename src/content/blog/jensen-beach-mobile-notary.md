## Why Jensen Beach Residents Need a Notary

- **A strong waterfront and coastal real estate market.** Deed transfers and closing documents are a steady need, particularly on Hutchinson Island.
- **A significant retiree and seasonal resident population.** Estate planning documents — powers of attorney, healthcare directives, trusts — come up regularly.
- **A close, community-oriented character**, where in-home appointments are often preferred over a trip to a bank or office.
- **Local small businesses**, particularly around Jensen Beach's commercial areas, generating contract and LLC notarizations.

## Documents Commonly Notarized in Jensen Beach

- Real estate deeds and closing documents
- Powers of attorney and healthcare surrogate designations
- Trust and estate planning documents
- Affidavits and sworn statements
- Business agreements and contracts
