Martin County's mix of historic waterfront communities, golf course neighborhoods, and a substantial retiree population creates steady, consistent demand for mobile notary services — and coverage extends throughout the county.

## The Full County, Covered

Mobile notary service reaches every part of Martin County:

- **Stuart**, the county seat and historic waterfront hub
- **Palm City**, with its established residential and golf communities
- **Jensen Beach**, spanning the mainland and Hutchinson Island
- **Hobe Sound**, toward the county's southern edge

## What It Costs

Martin County falls within the 30–60 mile travel tier from Vero Beach:

| Travel Fee | Per-Stamp Fee | Total for 1 Document |
|---|---|---|
| $75+ | $10 | $85+ |

## Why Martin County Keeps Notaries Busy

- **A significant retiree population** countywide, generating steady demand for powers of attorney, healthcare directives, and trust documents
- **An active waterfront and golf community real estate market**, keeping closing-related notarizations common
- **Seasonal residents**, handling paperwork before heading north or south for the season
- **Established small businesses**, particularly around Stuart's historic downtown

## Documents Commonly Notarized Countywide

- Powers of attorney and healthcare surrogate designations
- Real estate deeds and closing documents
- Trust and estate planning documents
- Affidavits and sworn statements
- Business agreements and contracts
