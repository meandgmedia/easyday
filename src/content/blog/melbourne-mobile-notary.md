Melbourne's position at the northern edge of the Treasure Coast mobile service area means residents here have two solid options: an in-person visit for documents that need one, or a Remote Online Notary session that skips the longer travel distance entirely.

## Coverage Throughout Melbourne and Brevard County

Mobile notary visits reach Melbourne and the surrounding Brevard County area, including Palm Bay, Satellite Beach, and nearby communities. Given the distance from Vero Beach, appointments here are typically scheduled a bit further ahead than closer areas, though same-day visits are still often possible.

## What It Costs

## Why Melbourne Keeps Notaries Busy

- **A large, active community.** As one of the bigger cities in the area, Melbourne generates a steady stream of everyday notarization needs.
- **Health First and area medical facilities.** Hospital bedside visits for medical and financial powers of attorney are a regular request.
- **An established real estate market.** Home purchases, refinances, and deed transfers keep closing-related notarizations common.
- **A strong small business and tech sector**, generating LLC agreements and business contract notarizations.

## Documents Commonly Notarized in Melbourne

- Powers of attorney and healthcare surrogate designations
- Real estate deeds and closing documents
- Hospital and bedside notarizations
- Business formation documents and agreements
- Affidavits and sworn statements
