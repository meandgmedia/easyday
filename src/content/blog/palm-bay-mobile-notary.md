## Why Palm Bay Keeps Notaries Busy

- **One of Brevard County's largest and fastest-growing cities.** Steady population growth keeps everyday notarization needs consistent.
- **Palm Bay Hospital and area medical facilities.** Hospital bedside visits for medical and financial powers of attorney are a regular request.
- **An active residential real estate market**, keeping closing-related notarizations common.
- **A growing small business and industrial sector**, generating LLC agreements and business contract notarizations.

## Documents Commonly Notarized in Palm Bay

- Hospital and bedside notarizations
- Business formation documents and agreements
- Affidavits and sworn statements
