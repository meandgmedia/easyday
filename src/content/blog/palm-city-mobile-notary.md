## Why Palm City Residents Need a Notary

- **Established residential and golf communities.** Estate planning documents — powers of attorney, healthcare directives, trusts — are a steady need among long-term residents.
- **An active local real estate market**, keeping deed transfers and closing notarizations common.
- **A retiree and part-time resident population**, similar to much of Martin County, generating regular estate planning appointments.
- **Local small businesses**, particularly around Palm City's commercial corridors, needing LLC and contract notarizations.

## Documents Commonly Notarized in Palm City

- Powers of attorney and healthcare surrogate designations
- Real estate deeds and closing documents
- Trust and estate planning documents
- Affidavits and sworn statements
- Business agreements and contracts
