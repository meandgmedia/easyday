## Why Port St. Lucie Keeps Notaries Busy

A few things about this city specifically drive consistent notary demand:

- **A hot real estate market.** Port St. Lucie has been one of the fastest-growing cities in Florida for years, which means a constant flow of closings, refinances, and deed transfers.
- **A large working population.** Plenty of PSL residents commute for work, making evening and weekend mobile appointments especially valuable.
- **A significant retiree community.** Estate planning documents — powers of attorney, trusts, healthcare directives — come up often.
- **Growing small business activity.** New LLCs and business agreements need notarized signatures regularly.

## Documents Commonly Notarized in Port St. Lucie

- Real estate deeds and closing documents
- Powers of attorney and healthcare surrogate designations
- Affidavits and sworn statements
- Business formation documents and contracts
- Travel consent forms
