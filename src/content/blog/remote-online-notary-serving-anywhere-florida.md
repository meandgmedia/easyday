If you've found this site while searching for a notary somewhere well outside the Treasure Coast — Orlando, Tampa, West Palm Beach, Jacksonville, anywhere else in Florida — here's the good news: distance from Vero Beach doesn't matter for Remote Online Notarization.

## Two Very Different Service Areas

[Easy Day Notary](/) actually offers two distinct kinds of service, with two very different geographic reaches:

- **Mobile notary visits** — limited to the Treasure Coast and surrounding areas, roughly Vero Beach out to about 60 miles in any direction, where an in-person visit is realistic
- **Remote Online Notary (RON)** — available to any signer physically located within Florida, regardless of how far that is from Vero Beach

If you're searching from a city that's clearly outside mobile driving distance, RON is exactly the service built for your situation.

## Why This Actually Works Well

Remote Online Notarization was built specifically to solve the geography problem. Since the entire session — identity verification, video meeting, electronic signing — happens through a secure online platform, your physical distance from any particular notary's home base simply doesn't factor into whether the service works. A signer in Pensacola and a signer in Key West can both use the exact same RON process with equally fast turnaround.

## What You'll Need, No Matter Where You Are in Florida

- **A device with a camera** — laptop, tablet, or smartphone
- **A stable internet connection**
- **A valid, government-issued photo ID**
- **Your unsigned document**, ready to sign during the session

## Documents That Work Well Through RON

Most everyday notarizations translate cleanly to a remote session — affidavits, powers of attorney (with witnesses joining the same call), business agreements, travel consent forms, and plenty more. Our guide on [documents you can notarize online](/blog/documents-you-can-notarize-online) covers the specifics of what typically works and what doesn't.

## No Travel Fee, Wherever You Are

Since there's no travel involved, RON pricing doesn't scale with your distance from Vero Beach the way mobile visits do — you pay the standard notarial fee with no separate travel charge, whether you're in Melbourne or Miami. Our [pricing guide](/blog/florida-mobile-notary-pricing) covers how mobile pricing works for comparison, though RON sidesteps that entire structure.
