## Why Satellite Beach Residents Need a Notary

- **An active barrier island real estate market.** Deed transfers and closing documents are a steady need for coastal properties.
- **A significant retiree and established resident population.** Estate planning documents — powers of attorney, healthcare directives, trusts — come up regularly.
- **A close-knit beach community**, where in-home appointments are often preferred over a trip off the island.
- **Patrick Space Force Base proximity**, generating notarization needs tied to military family situations, including powers of attorney before deployment.

## Documents Commonly Notarized in Satellite Beach

- Real estate deeds and closing documents
- Powers of attorney and healthcare surrogate designations
- Trust and estate planning documents
- Military-related powers of attorney
- Affidavits and sworn statements
