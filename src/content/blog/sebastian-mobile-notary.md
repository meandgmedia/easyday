## Why Sebastian Keeps Notaries Busy

- **A growing retiree community.** Estate planning documents — powers of attorney, healthcare directives, trusts — are a steady request.
- **Active residential growth.** Sebastian has seen consistent housing development, keeping real estate closing notarizations regular.
- **A tight-knit small business scene**, particularly around the riverfront and historic downtown areas.
- **Sebastian River Medical Center and area facilities**, generating occasional hospital bedside notarization requests.

## Documents Commonly Notarized in Sebastian

- Powers of attorney and healthcare surrogate designations
- Real estate deeds and closing documents
- Affidavits and sworn statements
- Trust and estate planning documents
- Business formation documents
