St. Lucie County has become one of the fastest-growing corners of Florida, anchored by Port St. Lucie's rapid expansion and Fort Pierce's historic waterfront core — and mobile notary coverage reaches every part of it.

## The Full County, Covered

Mobile notary service extends throughout St. Lucie County:

- **Port St. Lucie**, one of the largest and fastest-growing cities in the state
- **Fort Pierce**, the county seat and historic waterfront hub
- **Unincorporated areas** throughout the county
- **Surrounding communities** connected to both major cities

## What It Costs

Most of St. Lucie County falls within the 15–30 mile travel tier from Vero Beach:

| Travel Fee | Per-Stamp Fee | Total for 1 Document |

## Why St. Lucie County Keeps Notaries Busy

- **Explosive residential growth**, particularly in Port St. Lucie, keeping real estate closing notarizations a steady, high-volume need
- **A significant retiree population**, generating consistent demand for powers of attorney, healthcare directives, and trust documents
- **Lawnwood Regional Medical Center and other facilities**, generating hospital bedside notarization requests
- **A growing small business community**, particularly around Port St. Lucie's commercial development

## Documents Commonly Notarized Countywide

- Real estate deeds and closing documents
- Powers of attorney and healthcare surrogate designations
- Hospital and bedside notarizations
- Business formation documents
- Trust and estate planning documents
